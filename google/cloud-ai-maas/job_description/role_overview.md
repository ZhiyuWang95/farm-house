## Team mission — Vertex Prediction MaaS
Democratize access to state-of-the-art open- and closed-source models by
leveraging Google's advanced hardware infrastructure, turning them into scalable,
reliable, efficient services.

- Build infrastructure for model creators to monetize their models, including
  Anthropic, Meta, Mistral
- Work with partners (Anthropic, Mistral) to bring new models/features to the
  platform
- Build a platform for partners to manage their services on Vertex
- Work with sister teams on optimized models/serving techniques (e.g. prefix
  caching, cache-aware routing)
- Build infrastructure for efficiently onboarding open- and closed-source models
  to Google Cloud
- Scale and maintain existing Model-as-a-Service deployments

## Role summary
GenAI models (text, code, images, video, increasingly long context) are powerful
but hard for even technically strong teams to develop, scale, and manage
cost-effectively. MaaS provides an abstraction layer to host large GenAI models
(Anthropic, Llama, Mistral, Deepseek) as scalable cloud services, with tooling for
use, integration, and model management.
